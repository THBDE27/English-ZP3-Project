The power rack was invented by Jim Steen and Sam VanCura at Total Performance, In. in 1988. The first product, the Power Rack, had undergone over 75 improvements since its inception. Jim Steen, an All-American swimmer at Kent State University, has made history as coach, while Sam VanCura, a former swimmer and coach, was drawn to building something for swimming due to the opportunity it presented. The Power Rack has become a staple in swimming training, providing swimmers with a way to develop strength and power effectively.
