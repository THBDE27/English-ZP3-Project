There are many good brands of goggles on the market. The two mainstream manufacturers are currently Arena and Speedo. Based on a performance ranking, Arena holds the top spot with the “Arena Cobra Edge Swipe Goggle”. It is approximately 100$ and is the most advanced racing goggle to date. It has a massive peripheral vision with a swipe technology that holds up throughout the preliminaries and the finals. They are molded for speed and built for long meets. They are worn by many world record holders like Adam Peaty of Great Britain and Summer McIntosh of Canada.
